# Dissociate app with account in Google Play

1. Remove the account the app associated with from the device.
2. Clear data and cache of Google Play.
3. Now the apps associated with the removed account are re-associated with the main account.
4 Optionally re-login to the removed account.

Source:

Sidharthan Sudharman & Jared Rollins. 2019. Google Play Help. https://support.google.com/googleplay/thread/5166100?sjid=17254453073419228265-NC
